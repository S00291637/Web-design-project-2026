How mental arithmetic develops children?

It's not just about the ability to count quickly. It's a powerful tool for the harmonious development of both hemispheres of a child's brain.

Key Benefits of Learning

Concentration and memory

Lessons teach the child to focus on the task without being distracted by external stimuli. Abacus visualization develops photographic memory.

Academic success

The development of logic and thinking speed helps children master the school curriculum faster, not only in mathematics but also in other subjects.

Self-confidence

When a child realizes that they can solve complex problems faster than adults, their self-esteem grows, and this carries over to all areas of life.

Creative potential

Working with images activates the right hemisphere, allowing the child to find non-standard solutions to any problems.
