Information Section

01. About the Method

Mental arithmetic is a unique intelligence development system based on the use of the ancient abacus counting tool. During the learning process, children learn to visualize calculations, which activates the right hemisphere of the brain.

Why Choose Us?

  • Individual approach to every child
  • Certified educators with over 5 years of experience
  • Unique online platform for practice

02. Learning Process

The program is divided into difficulty levels. Each level lasts an average of 3-4 months. We don't just teach counting — we develop perseverance, photographic memory, and self-confidence. Our students become winners of international competitions. The exercise system is built from simple to complex, which eliminates stress for the child. Parents receive weekly progress reports.

Enrollment Steps

  1. Application on site
  2. Trial lesson
  3. Level testing
  4. Course payment
  5. Start of classes
